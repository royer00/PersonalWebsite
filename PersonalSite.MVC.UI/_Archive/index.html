Kevin Royer

WEB DEVELOPER | KANSAS CITY | royerkevin@outlook.com

I am a web developer based in Kansas City. I love all things programming and I've been studying full-stack development at Centriq Training. Previously, my entire professional career was spent in the service-industry. From server to General Manager, I learned a great-deal about customer-service, troubleshooting in a fast-paced environment, and the challenges of running a small business. Unfortunately, every day started to seem the same. I sought a new challenge in web-development and I haven't looked back! I love it. When I'm not learning or coding, I enjoy nerding out on my trombone and hanging out with my 17-year-old dog Mingus.

Jazz and Other Music

  • Green Lady Lounge - Presently the best place to hear live jazz in the city.
  • The American Jazz Museum - It's amazing. It also has an adjoining jazz club called "The Blue Room" (yes, they serve drinks)
  • Knuckleheads - A music venue with great blues and country.
  • BB's Lawnside BBQ - Great live blues and pretty good food too.
  • JazzAdvice - An amazing website for learning jazz improvisation.
  • Mills Record Company - A great selection of vinyl.
  • BAC Music - Making some of the best trombones in the world, right here in Kansas City.

Places to Eat and Drink
